    SUZUKI CONNECT

    SMARTWATCH CONNECTIVITY

    Experience seamless connectivity through your Smartwatch, truly enabling you to be closer to your car. It’s as convenient as it gets. Available to users on Android and iOS.

    BUZZER ON/OFF

    You can easily connect your Smartwatch to start and stop the engine, control the car’s AC, lock the doors, or even check the vehicle status, remotely.

    VEHICLE LOCK

    You can also check the vehicle status (fuel level, range*, location, door open/close, headlamp on/off, hazard switch, battery status etc.) remotely on your smartwatch. *Feature is available in selected models & variants only.

    HEADLAMP ALERT

    User will get notification on Smartwatch if the headlamps are on.

    OVER SPEEDING

    Get alerts/notifications on your Smartwatch in cases where your vehicle is being towed-away. If your car breaches the geofence, and if it overspeed.

    GEOFENCE

    Get notified if your car enters or exits the virtual boundary set by you.

    BATTERY STATUS

    User will get notification whenever vehicle battery is low.

    REMOTE OPERATIONS

    Experience remote connectivity with your car when you are away from it.

    REMOTE IMMOBILIZER REQUEST

    In case of a break-in, remotely prohibit the engine restart, restricting any further movement.

    REMOTE HAZARD

    Easily locate your Maruti Suzuki vehicle in a crowded parking space without facing any difficulty.

    VEHICLE HEALTH CHECK

    Get remote access to your vehicle status and all the information on its vital parameters.

    REMOTE AC (AT ONLY)

    User can remotely turn on/off the AC to set a convenient temperature before entering car.

    Access your Maruti Suzuki vehicle remotely using your voice with Suzuki Connect skill for Amazon Alexa.

    LOCATION

    With Suzuki Connect, get the location of your Maruti Suzuki vehicle through voice commands using supported Alexa-enabled devices.

    ODOMETER INFORMATION

    Keep a track of your vehicle and the distance travelled by it, using supported Alexa-enabled devices.

    DRIVE HISTORY AND INFORMATION

    User can access Past trip details of the vehicle using supported Alexa-enabled devices.

    VEHICLE TRACKING

    Suzuki Connect allows you to keep a track of your vehicle at all times offering you complete peace-of-mind.

    LIVE VEHICLE TRACKING

    Always stay informed and trouble-free by enabling location tracking and get real-time status of vehicle movement.

    SHARE VEHICLE LOCATION

    Share live car location with your family and friends allowing them to track your car’s movement and current location.

    SAFETY & SECURITY ALERTS

    Suzuki Connect sends you an array of alerts to make sure you are always at peace when it comes to the safety and security of your loved ones.

    EMERGENCY ALERT

    In the event of airbags being deployed, registered emergency contacts will be notified through SMS and Email.

    TOW AWAY ALERT

    In case your vehicle is towed away, an alert will be sent to your phone and your emergency contact. Screen reader support enabled.

    DESIGNED TO KEEP YOUR SAFETY IN MIND

    SUZUKI-TECt BODY

    Suzuki’s Total Effective Control Technology (TECT) concept results in effective absorption and dispersion of crash energy. The effective use of high-tensile steel for major parts makes the body light, strong and safe.

    SEAT BELT PRE-TENSIONERS WITH FORCE LIMITERS

    Pre-tensioners restrain the passengers and Force Limiters prevent seatbelt-inflicted injury.

    FULL FRONTAL IMPACT COMPLIANCE, FRONTAL OFFSET IMPACT COMPLIANCE, SIDE IMPACT COMPLIANCE

    Successfully tested and in compliance with international safety norms.

    PEDESTRIAN PROTECTION COMPLIANCE

    6 AIR BAGS
